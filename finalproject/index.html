Welcome Indiana County Humane Society employees!

This website is designed to help you learn about the Indiana County Humane Society daily tasks and procedures, you will be performing as part of the Dog Team. Check out the different pages outlining the steps for Kennel Cleaning, watch the kennel cleaning videos to see a demonstration, and view the Daily Morning Routine tasks in an interactive module! This training is intended to be self-paced and engaging so take your time and have fun!


The learning objectives for this training include:

  • From memory, ICHS employees will be able to identify the importance of properly completing the daily morning tasks for the dog kennel, with a minimum of 90% accuracy.
  • From memory, ICHS employees will be able to identify the consequences of both following and not following protocol, with a minimum of 90% accuracy.
  • From a list of tasks, ICHS employees will be able to identify all the daily morning tasks, with a minimum of 90% of tasks properly identified.
  • From a list of tasks, ICHS employees will be able to properly order the sequence of all the daily morning tasks, with a minimum of 90% accuracy.
  • From a list of supplies and tasks, ICHS employees will be able to match which supplies are needed to perform the kennel cleaning activity, with a minimum of 90% accuracy.
  • From a list of task steps, ICHS employees will be able to properly order the steps to complete the kennel cleaning activity, with a minimum of 90% accuracy.
